Author: Bhaskar Kumawat

Setup

Consider a fixed-size population consisting of NNN organisms, each with a genotype composed of LLL sites that can be in one of two states—0 or 1. Let kkk denote the number of 111s present in a particular genome, and nkn_knk​ is the number of organisms in the population with kkk 111s in their genome.
